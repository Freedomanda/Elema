REPORT ZZKURAC8 .

Tables : ZSB002 .

Data : tab TYPE TABLE OF ZSB002 ,
WRK TYPE ZSB002 .

Select-Options S_SPMON For zsb002-spmon .


PARAMETERS : M_ZVRSIO LIKE ZSB002-ZVRSIO ,
A_ZVRSIO LIKE ZSB002-ZVRSIO ,
P_PKUNAG LIKE ZSB002-PKUNAG .




Start-Of-Selection .


CLEAR : WRK .
REFRESH TAB .


SELECT * FROM ZSB002
INTO TABLE  TAB
WHERE SPMON IN S_SPMON
AND  ZVRSIO EQ M_ZVRSIO
AND PKUNAG  EQ P_PKUNAG
.

LOOP AT TAB INTO WRK .

MOVE A_ZVRSIO TO WRK-ZVRSIO .

MODIFY TAB FROM WRK .
ENDlOOP .

MODIFY ZSB002 FROM TABLE TAB .
